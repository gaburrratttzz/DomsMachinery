Doms Machinery

Machines help make work easier by performing tasks more efficiently. Explore different types of machines:

1. Simple Machines

Simple machines are basic devices that make work easier. They include levers, pulleys, inclined planes, and more. These devices multiply force or change its direction.

2. Electric Machines

Electric machines convert electrical energy into mechanical energy. They power devices like motors, fans, and drills, and are widely used in homes and industries.

3. Hydraulic Machines

Hydraulic machines use pressurized fluids to move objects. They’re commonly found in construction, automotive, and heavy machinery like jacks and lifts.

4. Mechanical Machines

Mechanical machines are powered by mechanical energy. They use gears, wheels, and levers to perform work, and are found in everything from cars to clocks.
